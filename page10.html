Design with others

Seminar aimed to do an intervention within a community with the values of soil and land

Soil

For the first class, we visited the Sanz neighborhood, specifically Can Batllo, where we learned about the transformative power inherent in collective action and community engagement, emphasizing a perspective of working directly with the community and social considerations. As part of the activity, we observed issues within the neighborhood to identify a thematic focus for our seminar work.

I was truly impressed by the experience, finding the work they have done to be admirable and inspiring. The class focused on the methodology of transformation and intervention.
