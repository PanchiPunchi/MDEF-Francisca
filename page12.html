Extended Intelligences

Seminar that aims to get more involved in the developing of artificial intelligences and the ways that can be use with a more human perspective 

Visit to the AI exhibition

At the beginning of the seminar, we visited the CCCB to explore an exhibition showcasing projects created with artificial intelligence. The projects exhibited various applications at artistic, practical, and problem-solving levels, providing insights into the multifaceted aspects of AI, coupled with immersive experiences

Final Project - Bingo AI


By the end of the seminar, as a group, we were tasked with developing an artificial intelligence. For this process, we had to search for datasets (in our case, related to garbage recognition), train a neural network, and implement code. The goal was not for the AI to function (due to time constraints), but rather to comprehend the creation process and identify the crucial points involved.

Reflexions 

The seminar was very comprehensive. Firstly, we appreciate the teachers' great concern and patience in helping us understand the material. Achieving an understanding of the human perspective behind the automation of a process opens the door to various questions and insights into the final outcomes. Recognizing that artificial intelligence is not a creative robot but rather a statistical process carried out by individuals with opinions and biases is crucial. Personally, the course was very gratifying, especially considering that I came with no practical background on the subject.
