Personal Reflections:

At the beginning, when one knows the final goal, the starting process can be overwhelming. Starting with a card game is a relaxed and playful way. It adds value for the creativity process of making connections with the cards and from learning through the perspectives and knowledge of the classmates

Then, with the design space, it provides a compassionate start to see a mental map of where the project path is leading and where I stand with my interests and possibilities.

It is a start to kickstart a machine of creativity and affection with the project to be carried out.
